How to Set it Up? --
1. Open thinkorswim.

2. Go to Charts, click on Studies and open the panel by clicking 'Edit studies'.

3. Click 'import', and navigate to the 'Crossing_MACD_IndicatorSTUDY.ts' file and click open.

4. You should now have your new study just make sure to add it and apply it to your charts.
